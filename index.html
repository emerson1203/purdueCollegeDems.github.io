College Democrats of Purdue

Welcome to College Democrats of Purdue! We are a recently re-formed club, after the original club was discontinued a few years ago. We're very excited to be back. Our main goal is educating Purdue students about current politics, United States government, and how to get more involved!

Meeting Schedule

Meetings take place every other week on [day of the week]'s, alternating between educational and action meetings.

Meetings are split into two types: educational meetings and action meetings. For educational meetings, members of the club vote on a topic to discuss and learn more about. We learn about topics such as local elections, checks and balances, and histories of political movements to provide club members with a better understanding of the functions of government. Action meetings are related to current events, allowing club members to get involved and make a difference. We attend protests, host tabling, and collaborate with other political clubs on campus.
